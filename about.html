About-Us

The Gram Seva Sansthan (GSS) is a voluntary and charitable, grass root level, development constructive organization. Integrated and sustainable development of the entire hilly terrain together with human resources, with the co-operation of the masses is the motto of the Gram Seva Sansthan GSS is registered as a Society under the Societies Registration Act (1860) and under the FCRA (1976) with the Ministry of Home Affairs. GSS has active grass root level rural integrated development social research and technical support and working programmes in Water Resource Management, Rural Organizations monitoring, Environment Education and protection, Child Environment, Social Evils Eradication and Dulit Upliftment.

MAIN OBJECTIVES OF THE ORGANIZATION

On the principles of village self-governance, building a self-dependent, equitable, cultured, and healthy, educated and decentralized society. To remove poverty, disease and socio-economic disparities are from the region and cultivate a spirit of enterprise, self-dependence and confidence among the community members, through bringing scientific temperament and developing capacity.

ACTIVITIES UNDERTAKEN

Since its’ inception, Gram Seva Sansthan worked on Nursery Development, Plantation, Balwadi programme, Mahila Shakti Programme, Women Empowerment Programme, Watershed Development Project, Health Awareness, Child Environmental Project, Disaster Relief, Inanagad Watershed Development Project, Social Networking & Lawyers Forums Establishment & Women Empowerment etc with funding support from Uttarakhand Seva Nidhi Almora, EFFICOR, New Delhi. Chirag, Nainital, IGSSS New Delhi, UPVHA Dehradun, FORRAD New Delhi, CIDA Canada, UNICEF Lucknow, SRTT Mumbai, Uttarakhand Govt, NOVIB Netherland, National Dalit Forum & Centre for World Solidarity Andhra Pradesh, NABARD Dehradun and THDC, Rishikesh.
